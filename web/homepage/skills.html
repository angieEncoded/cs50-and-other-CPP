On the front end:

ReactJS is my go-to framework for front end development. I like the simplicity and unopinionated design so I can focus more on the business logic itself and less on making the logic fit into the framework.

I use a combination of Bootstrap and custom css to bring projects to life. Twitter bootstrap is my favorite CSS library.

On the back end:

I work primarily with NodeJS on the back end. My projects are powered by Express servers and the ones that aren't using React are server-side rendered with EJS templates.

In the data:

All of my projects are powered by MariaDB. I've been an ardent follower of the project for years.
